TW-AnsCheck

Apr 2024 ~ Present

HTML JavaScript Bootstrap

An online platform for checking answers to Taiwan's entrance exams. Taiwan's entrance exam score reports only show grade levels or point scales, not raw scores. To find out their raw scores, students must check each answer one by one against the official answer key, which is time-consuming, inconvenient, and prone to mistakes in marking answers or calculating scores. This website was created to address these issues and streamline the process.
