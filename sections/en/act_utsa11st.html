University Of Taipei Student Association 11st

Organization Overview

The University Of Taipei Student Association is established in accordance with Article 33 of the University Act and Article 39 of the Taipei City University Organizational Guidelines. Its purpose is to promote campus democracy, advocate for and safeguard student rights, enhance students' learning experiences, and foster students' self-governance capabilities.
